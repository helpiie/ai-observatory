# Concepts

## Runtime Governance

Traditional AI governance often focuses on policies, documentation, and approval processes before deployment.

AI Observatory explores governance during execution.

The key question:

> Does a human still have meaningful control while an AI system is operating?

## Evidence of Control

A trustworthy AI system should provide evidence about:

- what decisions were made
- what information influenced those decisions
- what permissions were active
- whether intervention was still possible

## Control Degradation

Control may disappear gradually before a visible failure occurs.

Signals may include:

- shrinking intervention windows
- increasing correction costs
- missing context
- unclear decision ownership
